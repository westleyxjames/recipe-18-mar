Avocado Toast with Poached Egg

Trendy breakfast toast topped with creamy avocado and perfectly poached egg

Prep Time5 min
Cook Time8 min
Total Time13 min
Servings2
DifficultyEasy

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Nutrition Information

320Calories
14gProtein
28gCarbs
19gFat

Instructions

  1. Toast bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Fill a medium saucepan with 3 inches of water. Add vinegar and bring to gentle simmer.
  3. Crack each egg into small bowl. Create gentle whirlpool in water and slide egg into center.
  4. Cook 3-4 minutes for runny yolk, 5-6 for firmer yolk. Remove with slotted spoon.
  5. While eggs cook, mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread mashed avocado generously on toasted bread.
  7. Top each toast with a poached egg.
  8. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  9. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired. Serve immediately.

💡 Cooking Tips

  • Use very fresh eggs for best poaching results
  • Add everything bagel seasoning for extra flavor
  • Try adding sliced tomatoes or smoked salmon
  • Works great with sourdough or whole wheat bread
