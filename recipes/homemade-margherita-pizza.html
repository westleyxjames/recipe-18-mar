Homemade Margherita Pizza

Authentic Italian pizza with fresh mozzarella, basil, and tomato sauce

Prep Time 2 hours
Cook Time 12 min
Total Time 2h 15min
Servings 4
Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling

Nutrition Information

520 Calories
18g Protein
68g Carbs
18g Fat

*Per serving (serves 4)

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Mix flour and salt in a large bowl. Add yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Knead dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place in oiled bowl, cover, and let rise 1.5 hours until doubled.
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with pizza stone if available.
  6. Divide dough in half. Roll out on floured surface to 12-inch circles.
  7. Spread sauce on dough, leaving 1/2 inch border. Add mozzarella slices.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Top with fresh basil and drizzle with olive oil before serving.

💡 Cooking Tips

  • Let dough come to room temperature before rolling
  • Use a pizza stone for crispier crust
  • Don't overload with toppings
  • Dough can be made ahead and refrigerated overnight
