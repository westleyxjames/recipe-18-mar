Perfect Chocolate Chip Cookies

Classic homemade cookies with gooey chocolate chips

Prep Time15 min
Cook Time12 min
Total Time27 min
Servings24
DifficultyEasy

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Nutrition Information

195Calories
2gProtein
26gCarbs
10gFat

*Per cookie

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt in medium bowl.
  3. Beat butter and both sugars in large bowl until creamy, about 3 minutes.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, beat until well combined.
  5. Gradually stir in flour mixture until just combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips and walnuts if using.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough 2 inches apart on prepared sheets.
  8. Bake 9-12 minutes until golden brown around edges.
  9. Cool on baking sheet 2 minutes, then transfer to wire rack.
  10. Store in airtight container for up to 1 week.

💡 Cooking Tips

  • Chill dough for 30 minutes for thicker cookies
  • Use parchment paper to prevent sticking
  • Slightly underbake for chewier cookies
  • Freeze dough balls for fresh cookies anytime
