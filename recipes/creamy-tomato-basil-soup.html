Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Velvety smooth tomato soup with fresh basil and cream

Prep Time10 min
Cook Time30 min
Total Time40 min
Servings6
DifficultyEasy

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)

Nutrition Information

240Calories
4gProtein
18gCarbs
18gFat

*Per serving

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in large pot over medium heat. Sauté onion until soft, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Add fresh basil leaves and remove from heat.
  6. Blend soup using immersion blender until smooth, or blend in batches.
  7. Return to pot, stir in cream and butter.
  8. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar if needed. Heat through without boiling.
  9. Serve hot with grilled cheese sandwiches or crusty bread.

💡 Cooking Tips

  • Use San Marzano tomatoes for best flavor
  • Add a pinch of red pepper flakes for heat
  • Garnish with extra basil and a swirl of cream
  • Freezes well for up to 3 months (before adding cream)
