Garlic Butter Roasted Chicken

Juicy whole chicken roasted with garlic herb butter

Prep Time 15 min
Cook Time 1h 30min
Total Time 1h 45min
Servings 6
Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (4-5 lbs)
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
  • 1 lemon, halved
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, quartered

Nutrition Information

385 Calories
42g Protein
2g Carbs
22g Fat

*Per serving (serves 6)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Pat chicken dry with paper towels.
  2. Mix butter, garlic, rosemary, and thyme in a bowl.
  3. Carefully loosen skin from chicken breast and rub herb butter underneath.
  4. Season outside of chicken generously with salt and pepper.
  5. Place lemon halves and onion quarters inside cavity.
  6. Tie legs together with kitchen twine and tuck wings under.
  7. Roast for 20 minutes, then reduce heat to 375°F (190°C).
  8. Continue roasting for 1-1.5 hours until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  9. Let rest for 15 minutes before carving.

💡 Cooking Tips

  • Use a meat thermometer to ensure doneness
  • Baste every 30 minutes for extra crispy skin
  • Save the drippings to make gravy
  • Leftover chicken is perfect for sandwiches and salads
