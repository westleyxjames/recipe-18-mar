Traditional Beef Lasagna

Layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese

Prep Time30 min
Cook Time1 hour
Total Time1h 30min
Servings8
DifficultyMedium

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Nutrition Information

485Calories
32gProtein
36gCarbs
24gFat

Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef and sausage in large skillet until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add onion and garlic, cook 5 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, and basil. Simmer 20 minutes.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and lay flat.
  4. Mix ricotta, egg, and half the Parmesan in bowl.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Spread 1 cup meat sauce in bottom of 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer: 4 noodles, half the ricotta mixture, 1 cup mozzarella, meat sauce. Repeat.
  8. Top with remaining noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  9. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes. Remove foil, bake 25 more minutes until bubbly.
  10. Let stand 15 minutes before cutting. Garnish with parsley.

💡 Cooking Tips

  • Can be assembled a day ahead and refrigerated
  • Use no-boil noodles to save time
  • Let rest before cutting for cleaner slices
  • Leftovers freeze beautifully for up to 3 months
