Classic New York Cheesecake

Rich and creamy New York-style cheesecake with a graham cracker crust

Prep Time 20 min
Cook Time 1 hour
Total Time 5 hours
Servings 12
Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 32 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Nutrition Information

420 Calories
8g Protein
38g Carbs
28g Fat

*Per slice (serves 12)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 1/4 cup sugar. Press into bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Beat cream cheese and 1 1/4 cups sugar until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Mix in sour cream, vanilla extract, and flour until just combined.
  5. Pour filling over crust. Bake for 55-60 minutes until center is almost set.
  6. Turn off oven and leave cheesecake inside with door slightly open for 1 hour.
  7. Remove and cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Run a knife around edge before removing springform ring. Serve chilled.

💡 Cooking Tips

  • All ingredients should be at room temperature for best results
  • Use a water bath to prevent cracking
  • Don't open the oven door while baking
  • Top with fresh berries or fruit compote before serving
