Lemon Herb Grilled Salmon

Perfectly grilled salmon with fresh lemon and herbs

Prep Time10 min
Cook Time12 min
Total Time22 min
Servings4
DifficultyEasy

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 lemons (juice and zest)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Lemon slices for serving

Nutrition Information

340Calories
34gProtein
3gCarbs
21gFat

Instructions

  1. Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, garlic, dill, and parsley.
  2. Season salmon fillets with salt and pepper.
  3. Place salmon in shallow dish and pour marinade over. Let sit 15 minutes.
  4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat (375-400°F). Oil grill grates.
  5. Remove salmon from marinade, reserving marinade.
  6. Place salmon skin-side down on grill. Cook 5-6 minutes without moving.
  7. Carefully flip and cook another 4-5 minutes until fish flakes easily.
  8. Brush with reserved marinade during last minute of cooking.
  9. Serve immediately with lemon slices and extra fresh herbs.

💡 Cooking Tips

  • Don't overcook - salmon should be slightly pink in center
  • If skin sticks to grill, it needs more time
  • Works great in oven at 400°F for 12-15 minutes
  • Pairs wonderfully with roasted vegetables or rice
